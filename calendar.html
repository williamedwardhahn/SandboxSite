CLIP Art: Digital Haruspicy for Internet Studies DeepDream 2.0 is here! Explosive progress in cross-domain Self-Supervised Learning (SSL) has enabled a new class of generative technique for neural network interpretation: You provide a plain-English caption, and the network draws (from scratch) an image it thinks would appear near your text prompt in web pages it has seen around the internet. The long-range modeling capabilities of NLP transformers afford control over low-level structure, high-level concept relations, and tone/style, all captured from the internet and sprawled out over the canvas.
